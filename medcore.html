Features

Everything You Need

A complete ecosystem to digitize and streamline hospital operations from admission to discharge.

📋

Patient Records (EMR)

Centralized electronic medical records with complete patient history, prescriptions, lab results, and treatment plans — all searchable and secure.

📅

Appointment Scheduling

Smart scheduling with doctor availability, slot management, automated reminders, and walk-in queue management for seamless patient flow.

💳

Billing & Insurance

Automated invoice generation, insurance claim processing, payment tracking, and detailed financial reports for complete revenue cycle management.

💊

Pharmacy Management

Track drug inventory, manage prescriptions, automate reorder alerts, and maintain a complete audit trail for pharmacy operations.

🔬

Lab Integration

Seamless lab order management with result tracking, automatic report generation, and direct integration with diagnostic equipment.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Real-time insights on patient flow, revenue, department performance, doctor productivity, and operational efficiency — all in one view.
