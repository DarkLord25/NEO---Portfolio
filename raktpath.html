Our Transparency Goal

The Problem

Donation Without Visibility

Millions donate blood every year. But once donated, there's zero visibility into where it goes. RaktPath changes that.

01

No Post-Donation Tracking

Donors have no way to know if their blood was used, stored, discarded, or sent to another facility. The journey ends at donation.

02

Wastage Goes Untracked

Nearly 30% of collected blood is wasted due to expiry, mismanagement, or poor inventory — with no accountability system in place.

03

Donor Trust Erosion

Without transparency, repeat donation rates decline. Donors want to know their contribution made a real difference.

04

Fragmented Systems

Blood banks, hospitals, and testing labs operate on disconnected systems with no unified platform for tracking the complete lifecycle.

Solution

Complete Blood Lifecycle Tracking

Every unit of blood gets a unique ID. Every transition is logged. Every donor gets a transparent report.

🔖

Unique Blood Unit ID

Every donated unit receives a unique tracking code. Scan the QR or enter the ID to see real-time status from collection to transfusion.

📍

Real-Time Location Tracking

Know exactly where your blood is — at the blood bank, in testing, in cold storage, or being transported to a hospital in need.

🔬

Test Result Transparency

See when your blood was tested, what screenings it passed, and its current suitability status — all in your donor dashboard.

📱

Donor Dashboard

A personalized portal showing your donation history, blood unit journeys, impact reports, and upcoming donation eligibility dates.

🏥

Hospital Integration

Hospitals can request blood through the platform, and donors get notified when their specific unit is used in a life-saving procedure.

📊

Impact Reports

Receive a "Your Blood Saved a Life" notification with anonymized recipient details — bringing emotional closure and encouraging future donations.
